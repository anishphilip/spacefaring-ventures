Our Mission

Solve humanity's most important problems and accelerate the advent of an exciting future by investing in the world's best technology companies.

The name "Spacefaring" reflects our deepest conviction: that humanity's future lies beyond the boundaries of what is known today. For us, spacefaring is not merely about traveling beyond Earth, but about pursuing ventures as vast, open-ended, and limitless as space itself.

It represents the drive to push past every boundary, expand the frontiers of what is possible, and back the companies building technologies that will carry civilization forward - in space, intelligence, autonomy, robotics, energy, and other foundational technologies of the future.

Investment Thesis

First-principles conviction in converging technology waves

We identify generational founder-led companies at the intersection of autonomy, digital superintelligence, robotics, energy, and space - then back them with deep conviction and an extremely long time horizon that spans multiple decades.

◈

Frontier Technology

We invest in companies building at the edge of what is possible - technologies that expand the boundaries of human capability and reshape entire industries.

◆

Contrarian Conviction

Our investment approach emphasizes contrarian positions backed by rigorous first-principles analysis, held through volatility and skepticism until the thesis plays out.

Decades, Not Quarters

We think in decades, not quarters. The most consequential companies require patient capital and the willingness to endure short-term noise for long-term compounding.

Our Story

Over a decade of conviction investing

Spacefaring Ventures was born from a simple but powerful insight - that the most important technologies of our time were being systematically undervalued by mainstream investors.

2013

The Tesla bet

An early, high-conviction position in Tesla - years before the company's thesis was widely accepted. Held through nearly a decade of significant volatility and widespread skepticism, this experience shaped the investment philosophy that guides the firm today.

2020

Spacefaring Ventures founded

Built from the ground up as a solo GP - raising capital, making all investment decisions, managing LP relationships, and directing strategy independently. No institutional backing, no family wealth, no conventional VC credentials. Just first principles and conviction.

2021

SpaceX - first funds deployed

Launched the first fund vehicles to provide investors access to SpaceX equity on the secondary market. The first three funds, all 2021 vintage, have now been held for five years - a testament to patient, long-term conviction. The thesis: SpaceX was the single most important infrastructure company being built, and most investors were structurally locked out.

2025

xAI - expanding the frontier

Expanded into frontier AI with investments in xAI, recognizing the convergence between space infrastructure and digital superintelligence as the defining technology axis of the coming decades.

2026

SEC Exempt Reporting Adviser

Became a SEC Exempt Reporting Adviser, reflecting the firm's growth and increasing formalization of its compliance and reporting infrastructure as it scales across 11 fund vehicles and over 40 accredited investors worldwide to hundreds of millions of dollars in aggregate asset value across all fund vehicles.
