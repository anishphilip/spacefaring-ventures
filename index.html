Our Story

A decade of conviction investing

Spacefaring Ventures was born from a simple but powerful insight - that the most important technologies of our time were being systematically undervalued by mainstream investors.

2013

The Tesla bet

An early, high-conviction position in Tesla - years before the company's thesis was widely accepted. Held through significant volatility and widespread skepticism, this contrarian bet became the foundation for everything that followed.

2020

Spacefaring Ventures founded

Built from the ground up as a solo GP - raising capital, making all investment decisions, managing LP relationships, and directing strategy independently. No institutional backing, no family wealth, no conventional VC credentials. Just conviction.

2021

SpaceX - first funds deployed

Launched the first fund vehicles to provide investors access to SpaceX equity on the secondary market. The first 3 to 5 funds, all 2021 vintage, have now been held for five years - a testament to patient, long-term conviction. The thesis: SpaceX was the single most important infrastructure company being built, and most investors were structurally locked out.

2025

xAI - expanding the frontier

Expanded into frontier AI with investments in xAI, recognizing the convergence between space infrastructure and artificial intelligence as the defining technology axis of the coming decades.

2026

SEC Registered Adviser

Became a registered SEC Exempt Reporting Adviser, reflecting the firm's growth and commitment to institutional-grade compliance as it scales across 11 fund vehicles and over 40 investors worldwide.

Founder

Anish Philip

Founder, CEO, and sole General Partner of Spacefaring Ventures. Anish built the firm from the ground up as a solo GP - raising capital, making every investment decision, managing LP relationships, and directing strategy entirely alone.

His investing journey began in 2013 with an early, high-conviction position in Tesla - years before the company's thesis was widely accepted. That contrarian bet, held through significant volatility and widespread skepticism, became the foundation for his broader approach: first-principles analysis of converging technology waves across autonomy, AI, robotics, energy, and space.

Anish combines nearly two decades of software engineering experience at world-class technology companies with deep technical fluency that informs every investment decision. A first-generation immigrant, he built his career, his fund, and his track record independently - without family wealth, institutional networks, or conventional credentials in finance or venture capital.
