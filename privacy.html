Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

Spacefaring Ventures LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at spacefaring.ventures. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit our site, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information. We believe in transparency and minimal data collection.

Information We Collect

We designed this website to collect as little personal information as possible. Here is what our site and its services may process:

Infrastructure logs (Cloudflare). Our website is delivered through Cloudflare's content delivery network. As part of standard CDN and DNS operations, Cloudflare may temporarily process visitor IP addresses for security, performance optimization, and country-level analytics. Cloudflare discards raw IP addresses after deriving aggregate metrics. This processing is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy, not ours.

Cloudflare Web Analytics. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate visitor traffic (page views, device types, browsers, and country-level geography). This service does not use cookies, does not track individual users across sites, and does not store personally identifiable information. It is designed to be privacy-first and compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations by default.

Information We Do Not Collect

To be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not use tracking cookies or any cookies for analytics purposes
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party tracking scripts
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal information through the website
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any visitor data with third parties
  • We do not build visitor profiles or track users across websites
  • We do not use fingerprinting or any other persistent identification methods

How We Use Information

The limited information described above is used solely to operate and improve our website - specifically, to ensure the site loads quickly and securely (via Cloudflare) and to understand aggregate traffic patterns (via Cloudflare Web Analytics).

Cookies

Our website does not set any first-party cookies. Cloudflare may set a minimal technical cookie (such as __cf_bm) for bot detection and security purposes. This is a functional cookie required for site operation, not a tracking cookie. No consent banner is needed because we do not use cookies for analytics or advertising.

Third-Party Services

Our website relies on the following third-party services:

  • GitHub Pages - website hosting
  • Cloudflare - CDN, DNS, security, and privacy-first web analytics
  • Google Fonts - web font delivery (Outfit and Lato typefaces)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data. We encourage you to review their policies if you have concerns.

Data Retention

Cloudflare Web Analytics data is retained for up to 6 months in aggregate form. We do not maintain any long-term records tied to individual visitors.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not constitute an offer to sell or a solicitation of an offer to buy any security. If you choose to contact us separately via email or other channels regarding investment opportunities, any personal information you provide in that context is handled in accordance with our investor communications policies and applicable securities regulations - not this website privacy policy.

Your Rights

Because we collect virtually no personal information through this website, there is generally no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete. If you believe we hold any information about you and wish to exercise your rights under applicable privacy laws (including CCPA, CPRA, or GDPR), please contact us and we will respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
